Wedding Photography

At Brandon Adam Photography, the most important thing to us is that you have the best experience possible. We pride ourselves in our unsurpassed quality of services and products. We will capture every moment of laughter, joy, love, and beauty. Once the day is over, you’ll only be left with photos. We guarantee those photos will be everything you ever imagined and turn into one of your most cherished possessions.

Consultation, Planning, and the Investment

Once you reach out to us, we’ll set up a date to have your consultation. During the meeting you’ll choose your package and whether or not you’d like a second photographer. The consultation is also used to collect the retainer and officially reserve your date! Even if you weren’t set on booking, we’d love to meet with you to answer any questions you have and showcase our products.

Your packages start at $1795. Our high end offers packages that include canvases prints, albums, an engagement session, eight hours of coverage, and the rights to print the photos. Our basic package offers eight hours of coverage and the rights to print, and our other packages offer a balance between them.

Engagement Session

During the consultation, we’ll discuss the details of your engagement session, provided you want a package that includes it. We’ll set up a date and pick a location at your convenience. You will also have your engagement photos within two weeks of your session. You’re free to use the photos for social media, save the dates cards, or invitations!

The engagement session is always a great icebreaker to familiarize you with how we work. Having already worked with us previously, you’ll be much more comfortable on your wedding day. The phrase we always use here is “playing not posing.” It’s really important to us that all of your photos look fun and natural. Incorporating movement is the best we have found to keep people comfortable and get rid of the nerves.

Wedding Day

On your wedding day, we’ll come dressed to the nines and ready to go. You can expect between five hundred to one thousand photos from your wedding. With years of experience and dozens of weddings under our belts, you can be assured that nothing will be missed.

Photo Delivery and Album Design

You will have digital copies of your photos within two weeks of your wedding! Once we design the album, it will be proofed to you for approval. Upon approval it will be order and shipped. From your digitals you’ll also choose which photo you want printed on your canvas. Albums and canvases will generally take three to four weeks to get back from the lab. Your products will either be shipped directly to you or hand delivered! Along with the products your ordered, every couple gets a few wedding presents from us!
