Pet Photography

Pets are undoubtedly important members of your family. Our pet photography sessions are easy going for everyone. Your pet will love all the attention and treats they are getting. No matter how bad of a listener your pet may be, we’ll still be able to get great photos. By their very nature, pet photos are always candid. We will be down on the ground with them while they run around and play. Keeping your pet on leash is fine too! We are happy to edit out leashes and collars for any photos you’d like to order.

After your session, we will meet up on a separate day to order your photos! We offer boutique style products such as photo prints, canvases, and metal prints. All of our prints are archival and will last a hundred years or more. You will be able to cherish these photos for the rest of your life, and they won’t even fade.
