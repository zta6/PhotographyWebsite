Senior Portraits

I strive to offer the best senior portrait experience around. This is a pivotal point in you or your child's life and there is no better way to commemorate that than with photos!

As a parent, you no doubt have moments that you ask yourself "where has all the time gone? They grew up so fast!" Luckily, photography has the ability to freeze time and allow us to revist a moment for years and years to come. Their junior or senior year is the last time you'll have with them before they leave the nest. We want to help you capture a few momments in time that you can keep forever.

I enjoy senior portraits more than any other type of photography I do. It allows for so much more freedom when compared to the other genres I work in. Whether you’re a dancer, a football player, or a bookworm, the photos will capture your personality. I stay far away from the traditional approach. You will not hear “can you tilt your head to the right?” from me. I love getting people moving and having fun. It’s the best way to get genuine expressions and it prevents the posed look. During your shoot, we will make sure you are comfortable and having a blast. You’re welcome to bring changes of clothes and your pets! You can except between forty to a hundred photos from your senior session!

After your session, we will meet up on a separate day to order your photos! We offer boutique style products such as photo prints, canvases, and metal prints. We also offer canvas wall arrangements! The average investment for senior portraits is around $750 but there is no minimum purchase. All the prints are archival and will last a hundred years or more. They will be something you can pass down to the next generation. All of my sales are done in person. This ensures that none of your questions go unanswered and you get exactly what you wanted.
